Summer Mathematics Foundation Program
Terms & Conditions

1. Admission & Seat Confirmation

  • Admission is confirmed only after payment of the required booking amount.
  • Seats are limited and allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • No additional seats will be created once a batch is full.

2. Fee Structure & Payment Policy

  • Course fee depends on the selected batch size.
  • 30%–50% advance payment is required to reserve the seat.
  • Remaining fee must be paid on or before 10 May.
  • Failure to complete payment may result in cancellation of admission.

3. Refund Policy

  • The booking amount is non-refundable as the seat is reserved exclusively.
  • No refund will be provided after confirmation of admission.
  • If the program is cancelled by the institute, full refund will be issued.

4. Batch Structure

  • The program duration is 2 months.
  • Batch sizes ( 2, 5, or 8 students) will remain fixed.
  • Batch timing once allotted cannot be changed unless mutually agreed.

5. Attendance & Discipline

  • Students are expected to attend classes regularly.
  • No separate compensatory classes will be provided for missed sessions.
  • Students must maintain proper discipline during online classes.

6. Study Material & Tests

  • Practice sheets, assignments, and weekly tests will be provided.
  • Performance updates may be shared with parents when required.

7. Technical Requirements

  • Students must ensure stable internet connection and proper device setup.
  • The institute is not responsible for technical issues from the student’s side.

8. Code of Conduct

  • Misconduct or disruption may result in removal from the program without refund.

9. Program Objective

  • The program aims to strengthen conceptual understanding in Mathematics.
  • Results depend on student effort, practice, and discipline.

By enrolling in the Summer Mathematics Foundation Program, the student and parent agree to all the above terms and conditions.
