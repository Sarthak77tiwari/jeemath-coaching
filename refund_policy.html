Refund Policy

Please read this policy carefully before enrolling in any course at JEE Math Coaching. It explains how our demo classes, fees, and cancellations work.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Demo Class

  • Students are encouraged to attend the demo class before enrolling.
  • The demo class allows students and parents to evaluate the teaching methodology before making any payment.

2. No Refund After Enrollment (Important)

IMPORTANT: After attending the demo class, payment of the first month's fee confirms your admission. This fee is non-refundable. However, subject to institute approval, it may be adjusted toward another eligible batch or course. By making payment, you acknowledge that you have read and accepted this Refund Policy.

Policy:

  • Once the student has attended the demo class and chooses to enroll by paying the first month's fee, the payment is considered confirmation of admission.
  • The first month's fee is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • This amount cannot be refunded due to change of mind, lack of interest, schedule issues, personal reasons, or any other reason.

3. Fee Adjustment Policy

  • If a student decides to change to another batch, course, or subject offered by JEE Math Coaching (subject to availability and approval), the amount already paid may be adjusted.
  • Adjustment is allowed only at the sole discretion of the institute.
  • Cash refunds will not be provided.

4. Cancellation by Student

  • Students may discontinue classes at any time.
  • However, fees already paid for the current month are non-refundable.
  • Future months' fees need not be paid if the student discontinues.

5. Cancellation by Institute

  • If the institute cancels a course before it begins and no alternative batch is available, an appropriate refund or fee adjustment may be considered.
  • If an alternative batch is available, the student may be shifted instead of receiving a refund.

6. Technical Issues

  • Refunds will not be granted for internet problems, power outages, device issues, or technical problems at the student's end.
  • Reasonable support will be provided whenever possible.
We recommend a stable internet connection and a tested device before every class to avoid disruptions.

7. Payment Confirmation

By making payment, students and parents acknowledge that they have:

  • Read the Refund Policy.
  • Understood the policy.
  • Agreed to all terms and conditions.
