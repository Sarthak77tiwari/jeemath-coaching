// 02 — Curriculum

Complete Syllabus Coverage

Comprehensive preparation for JEE Mains and Advanced mathematics.

JEE Mains
  • Algebra — Complex Numbers, Quadratic Equations, Sequences & Series
  • Calculus — Limits, Continuity, Differentiation, Integration
  • Trigonometry — Ratios, Identities, Equations, Inverse Functions
  • Coordinate Geometry — Lines, Circles, Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola
  • Vectors & 3D Geometry
  • Probability & Statistics
JEE Advanced
  • Advanced Calculus — Differential Equations, Area Under Curves
  • Advanced Algebra — Matrices, Determinants, Complex Analysis
  • Advanced Coordinate Geometry — Conic Sections (Deep Dive)
  • Vector Algebra & 3D Applications
  • Combinatorics & Probability Theory
  • Mathematical Reasoning & Logic
Problem Solving
  • Previous Year Questions (PYQs) Analysis
  • Pattern Recognition & Shortcut Techniques
  • Time Management Strategies
  • Mock Tests & Performance Review
  • Concept Application in Real Problems
  • Exam Strategy & Tips
