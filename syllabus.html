2-Month Structured Program

Full Syllabus & Learning Roadmap

Every week has a clear goal. Every chapter builds on the last. Complete, purposeful, and exam-focused.

Month 01

Foundation Building

Week 01

Sets, Relations & Functions — Basics

Types of sets, Cartesian products, domain & range, types of functions

Week 02

Advanced Functions & Graphs

Composition, inverse, odd/even, modulus, signum, floor functions & graph sketching

Week 03

Trigonometry, Identities & Equations

All identities, compound angles, multiple angles, heights & distances

Week 04

Complex Numbers & Quadratic Equations

Argand plane, polar form, roots of unity, nature of roots, Vieta's formulas

Month 02

Application + Higher Level

Week 05

Sequence & Series, Binomial Theorem

AP, GP, HP, AGP, telescoping sums; binomial expansion, general term, middle term

Week 06

Permutation, Combination & Probability

Counting principles, circular arrangements, conditional probability, Bayes' theorem

Week 07

Coordinate Geometry — Lines, Circles & Basics

Family of lines, locus, standard conic sections, tangent & normal to circles

Week 08

Limits, Derivatives & Introduction to Vectors

ε–δ intuition, standard limits, first-principles differentiation, dot & cross product basics
