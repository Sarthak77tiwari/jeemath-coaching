CBSE Curriculum Blueprint

Class 10 Maths Syllabus & Unit-Wise Coverage

Our curriculum matches the official CBSE Class 10 Mathematics syllabus entirely, integrating NCERT solutions with previous years' questions (PYQs).

Unit 1: Number Systems & Algebra

  • Real Numbers: Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic, irrationality proofs.
  • Polynomials: Zeros of a polynomial, relationship between coefficients and quadratic zeros.
  • Linear Equations & Quadratics: Pair of Linear Equations in Two Variables, Quadratic Equations, and Arithmetic Progressions (AP).

Unit 2: Geometry & Trigonometry

  • Coordinate Geometry: Distance formula, section formula.
  • Triangles & Circles: Geometric proofs, similarity theorems, and tangent properties.
  • Trigonometry: Introduction to Trigonometric Ratios, trigonometric identities, and heights and distances (Applications of Trigonometry).

Unit 3: Mensuration, Statistics & Probability

  • Surface Areas & Volumes: Surface areas and volumes of combinations of solids.
  • Statistics & Probability: Mean, median, and mode of grouped data; classical probability definitions.

Parent Queries & Insights

Frequently Asked Questions: Class 10 CBSE Maths Tuition

Get immediate, transparent answers regarding our online batch sizes, teaching methodology, and board exam preparation strategies.

What is the maximum student batch size for Class 10 online math classes?

Our standard Batch Classes are strictly capped at a maximum of 3 students per batch. This micro-batch layout guarantees direct teacher access, allowing our educators to identify individual conceptual gaps and answer every doubt during the live session itself.

How does the coaching manage both CBSE school boards and JEE foundation skills?

We use a dual-teacher specialist model to balance both goals. Simran Ma'am leads the core CBSE Class 10 Board syllabus focusing on NCERT alignment, conceptual proofs, and marking-scheme presentation. Sarthak (M.Sc. Mathematics, IIT Hyderabad) provides an integrated problem-solving track that introduces advanced matrix theory, logical reasoning, and algebraic shortcuts required to build a solid foundation for eleventh-grade JEE preparation.

What specific materials are covered in the Class 10 Maths curriculum?

The program ensures 100% mastery of the official NCERT textbook exercises and exemplars. Additionally, we solve Chapter-Wise Previous Year Questions (PYQs) from the past 10 years of CBSE board exams, alongside higher-order thinking skills (HOTS) questions to prepare students for competency-based exam patterns.

How are student evaluation, testing, and progress tracking managed?

Students take a mandatory, structured test at the end of every week to assess chapter retention. Following the evaluation, detailed digital progress reports covering test scores, individual topic weaknesses, and batch attendance are shared directly with parents to ensure total academic transparency.

What happens if a student misses a live online mathematics class or has a doubt later?

Every live interactive session is fully recorded, providing students with immediate access to archive playbacks for revision. For daily homework queries, students have 24/7 access to a monitored WhatsApp doubt-clearance channel where our educators resolve academic questions within the same day.
