🔗 Sets & Relations Medium
Let A = {1, 2, 3, 4} and R = {(a, b) : b = a + 1}. Is R reflexive? Symmetric? Transitive?
💡 Hint: Check each property. For transitive, find a counterexample.
Not reflexive (1,1)∉R • Not symmetric (1,2)∈R but (2,1)∉R • Not transitive (1,2),(2,3)∈R but (1,3)∉R
📊 Functions Medium
Find domain of f(x) = 1/√(x² − 5x + 6). Is it one-one and onto?
💡 Hint: Denominator → inside √ must be > 0. Factorize (x−2)(x−3) > 0.
Domain: (−∞, 2) ∪ (3, ∞). Not one-one, not onto.
📐 Trigonometry Hard
Prove cos3x = 4cos³x - 3cosx. Then find cos20° × cos40° × cos80°.
💡 Hint: Use cos(2x+x) identity. Product value = 1/8.
cos20° × cos40° × cos80° = 1/8 (JEE Mains 2023 question)
